Frequently Asked Questions

Direct answers to the questions we hear most. No hedging. No ambiguity.

Fractional means you get executive-level expertise — a CFO, a COO, or both — without the full-time salary commitment. We embed inside your business part-time, working on the strategic and operational challenges that require senior leadership. You get the impact of a C-suite hire at a fraction of the cost. Our engagements are 6–12 months minimum because that's how long it takes to genuinely transform a business's financial and operational infrastructure.
We work with SMBs in the $500K to $7M revenue range — businesses that have outgrown their accountant but aren't ready for full-time executive hires. This is the stage where the gap between financial strategy and operational execution is most dangerous, and where our integrated approach delivers the highest impact. We maintain a deliberately small client roster at any time to ensure deep, personal engagement.
After a discovery call, we can typically begin within 1–2 weeks for sprints and consulting engagements. Fractional and integrated engagements may take slightly longer to scope properly. We believe in starting right rather than starting fast — but we don't waste time.

But the operator skill set and BI platform translate across any growth-stage SMB in the $500K–$7M range. The underlying problems are the same: financial architecture that doesn't support growth, operational systems that break under scale.

A Strategic Sprint is a single-project, time-boxed engagement with a defined scope and deliverable — build a cash flow forecast, design an ops playbook, implement a KPI dashboard. There's no ongoing commitment. A fractional retainer (CFO, COO, or Integrated) is an ongoing embedded engagement, minimum 6–12 months, where we function as your executive team. Sprints are the on-ramp. Retainers are the destination.
