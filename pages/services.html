Financial Leadership

Fractional CFO

Your financial architecture is the gap. You need someone who can build the model that tells you when to hire, design the BI infrastructure that connects your numbers to your decisions, and own the forward-looking financial strategy of your business. Not a CPA closing last year's books — an operator building next year's foundation.

Financial Planning & Forecasting

Forward-looking financial models that tell you when to hire, when to invest, and when to hold. 13-week cash flow forecasts, rolling budgets, scenario analysis.

Cash Flow Management

Real-time visibility into cash position, runway, and burn rate. Timing-based forecasting that prevents the surprises that kill growing businesses.

Strategic Budgeting

Budgets built around growth objectives — not last year's actuals. Departmental allocation, capital planning, and investment prioritization.

Executive Financial Reporting

Decision-grade reporting through our proprietary BI platform. Real-time dashboards covering revenue, margins, unit economics, and KPIs.

Profitability & Margin Analysis

Client-level, project-level, and service-level margin analysis. Identify where you're making money and where you're leaving it on the table.

Operational Leadership

Fractional COO

Your organization is the gap. The team is growing faster than the systems can handle. Delivery is inconsistent. The hiring plan doesn't connect to the financial plan. Kylie doesn't tell you what to build. She builds it with you.

Process Optimization

Audit and redesign operational workflows. Eliminate bottlenecks, reduce waste, and build repeatable processes that scale with growth.

Systems & Automation

Implement the technology stack and SOPs that let your team operate at 2x capacity without 2x headcount. Project management, CRM, delivery systems.

KPI Frameworks

Define and track the operational metrics that matter. Team utilization, delivery quality, capacity planning, and performance benchmarks.

Leadership & Team Operations

Hiring sequencing, team structure design, leadership development, and culture-building. Build the organization that can execute your growth plan.

Premium Tier

Integrated CFO & COO

The full picture. The full team. Strategy and execution, coordinated by design — because your CFO and COO share a mission, a household, and identical incentives.

Financial Strategy Meets Operational Execution

When Matt recommends a structural investment, Kylie has already modeled the operational capacity. When Kylie identifies a team gap, Matt has the financial runway mapped. No coordination risk. No communication lag.

Compounding Returns

The integrated model delivers outsized returns because financial decisions and operational decisions reinforce each other. Strategy without execution is a document. Execution without strategy is chaos. We eliminate both failure modes.

The Qera Proof Point

We embedded both functions in a multi-entity agency and scaled it to multi-million-dollar operations in 18 months. Financial infrastructure and organizational structure, built in parallel. That's the model.

Zero Direct Competitors

No other firm nationally offers a husband-wife CFO & COO team with a proprietary BI platform spanning both financial and operational data. This is a category of one.
